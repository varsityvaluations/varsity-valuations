The problem

The collective knows
what you're worth.
Do you?

Collectives have been writing NIL deals for years. They know what players at your position, your star rating, and your conference are signing for. They know your leverage before you walk in the room.

You don't. And that information gap costs athletes real money every single cycle.

Agents exist to close that gap — but they charge 15–20% of deal value to do it. On a $200K deal, that's $30–40K annually coming out of your pocket for information that should be accessible to any player willing to look for it.

Varsity Valuations gives you that information directly. Real verified deal comps from real athletes in your exact situation. Know your market value before anyone puts a number on the table.
